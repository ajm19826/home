General Upload Rules for SmartSchool:

To ensure a smooth submission process and increase the likelihood of approval, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Educational Relevance: The website must provide clear educational value and align with the SmartSchool curriculum or learning objectives.
  • Age Appropriateness: Content must be suitable for the intended age group of SmartSchool users. Avoid content that is violent, sexually suggestive, or promotes harmful activities.
  • Technical Requirements: The website must be functional, accessible, and compatible with common web browsers. Ensure that all links and interactive elements work correctly.
  • Copyright Compliance: Only submit websites that you own or have the legal right to distribute. Respect copyright laws and avoid using copyrighted material without permission.
  • No Malicious Content: Websites containing malware, viruses, or other harmful software will be immediately rejected.
